Harneys

I joined Harney Westwood & Riegels LLP as a Contract Express specialist, converting complex legal & government forms into human-readable documents. After six months, I transitioned into IT, becoming lead Operations Engineer for the London, Luxembourg and Cyprus offices, and providing support for the Hong Kong, British Virgin Islands & Cayman Islands offices.

I served as a fixture of the IT department and assisted staff in all twelve of our office sites through the ticketing system and on the phone. I led deployment of multiple IT systems, and wrote various papers on how to improve processes.

In this role I took ownership of Airwatch and on-site biometric security. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I supported Horizon virtual desktop infrastructure, distributed home office hardware, and was first point of contact for new user inductions across the three European offices.

I left Harneys in August 2022 to briefly join The Game Band, returning in 2024.
