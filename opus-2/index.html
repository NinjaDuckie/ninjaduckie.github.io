Opus 2

I started at Opus 2 in 2012 as a temp. Within half a year I was in their technical team, delivering hardware solutions for court and arbitration transcriptions, and helping pioneer the prototype of their flagship product, Magnum, a unified legal proceedings platform for evidence, arguments, transcripts, comms, etc.

In the five years that followed, I prepared the Singapore office and trained staff in the Singapore supreme court, and provided on-site assistance at private arbitration sites including Osaka, Miami, Geneva, the Hague, and even St. Vincent & the Grenadines.

When I was not globetrotting or working in the courts, I provided internal IT support, assisted with production and delivery of printed and digital transcripts, produced 3D marketing videos to demonstrate new product solutions, and kept morale up running after-hours tabletop games nights midweek.

I departed Opus 2 in 2017 when I was headhunted by a different firm.
