Discworld MUD

This free-to-play text-based adventure game set in the mostly-flat, circular world-supported-by-elephants-on-a-space-turtle conceived by the late Sir Terry Pratchett has been running since 1992. I had the privilege of becoming a member of the development team in 2023 and have been learning the aging C# systems for creating content therein.

The development team is incredibly encouraging and welcoming to new developers, and the community still thrives after 30 years, with things like Kefka's map & item database (left, the map of Ankh-Morpork) or Quow's dedicated MUSHclient package replete with various plugins that allow the playerbase to engage with a number of QoL improvements.

I plan to contribute to this project for as long as it is operational.
