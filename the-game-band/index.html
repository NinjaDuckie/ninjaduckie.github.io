The Game Band

I had been doing volunteer work with Blaseball from September of 2020; in August of 2022, I quit my existing job, and by December I was working for TGB on contract.

Together with the six other paid members of the community team, and 21 volunteers, we successfully wrangled the whims of 30,000+ people in a Discord server, fostering communication skills and a caring attitude in the community as a whole while still maintaining an individual competitive spirit for each of the 24 teams.

While in this role I worked with a Discord bot called Carl, personally setting up over 120 unique Tagscript snippets enabling members to interact with the bot and each other. I also supplemented existing graphics by producing Discord "banners" in Photoshop, highlighting recent important events in the game or community.

Blaseball was cancelled on June 2nd, 2023, and I moved back to IT work.
