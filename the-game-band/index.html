The Game Band

I had been doing volunteer work with Blaseball from September of 2020; in August of 2022, I quit my existing job, and by December I was working for TGB on contract. The job was much the same as my volunteer work, but now I had defined hours on a rota with coworkers, and was being paid for the community work I already knew how to do.

Together with the six other paid members of the community team, and the 21 volunteers, we successfully wrangled the whims of 30,000+ people in a Discord server, fostering communication skills and a caring attitude in the community as a whole while still maintaining an individual competitive spirit for each of the 24 teams.

While in this role I did some simple programming with a Discord bot called Carl, personally setting up over 120 unique Tagscript interactions by which members could interact with the bot and each other, to fill in time when the game wasn't running.

I also supplemented existing graphics by producing Discord "banners" in Photoshop, highlighting recent important events in the game or community.

When Blaseball was cancelled on June 2nd, 2023, I was let go from TGB in a mass purge that hit 75% of the staff.
